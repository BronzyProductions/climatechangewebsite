Will You Just Watch?

Support Those Who Need It

"No-one will blame you for global warming..except your children"-Unknown

Global warming is bound to affect all of us in its own way, even if its just a warm winter and cold summer. But we tend to forget about those who have lost almost everything in fires, floods, tsunamis and hurricanes of the past year. Once these tragic events are over and the pictures are no longer on the news we are left blank, we make the assumption all is ok. But the truth is these horrific events are long from over once the camera stops. In the Californian fires of 2018, ultimately called "The Campfire" many are still re-building their homes and lives. Many of them had to spend Christmas homeless, with friends or family or in emergency housing. We hope that you can hear their stories out and donate whatever you can.

We need your help to support the work of a group of scientists who may have found a permanent solution to atmospheric carbon dioxide (CO2). Their budget has been wiped out by recent cuts in federal funding for climate research, and they need your help to get back on track.

The effects of climate change are already life-threatening, and will only endanger more of us as time goes on. The biggest driver behind climate change is atmospheric carbon dioxide [3], a little molecule which is a by-product of almost everything we do, from breathing to driving cars.
