Who Are We

"I Like Skyrim"-Isaac Hall/2019

We are the Dead Planet Society. We setup the organization to inform people about the rising danger of global warming and mass climate change in the 21st century. We believe that everyone should recieve some form of education on climate change. The website was started by two guys, Isaac Hall and Stephen Flynn, given a deadline they built this website from scratch on the most important topic they could think of, global warming. They started by doing research on the topic and laying down foundations for the site. Now the website is visited by 20m people a day.

THE TEAM

Making A Difference

4Ocean

Ocean Cleaning

Alex Schulze and Andrew Cooper are two surfers who founded 4Ocean when they were on a surfing trip and realized the amount of pollution that was washing up on beautful beaches around the US and Australia. They started collecting plastic from the ocean with fleats of ships around the world and sell wrist bands made out of the material.

Tesla

Electric Cars

Well would it really be a project with me without a mention of Tesla? The company was setup in the early 2000's and was joined by Elon Musk shortly after its founding. Its aim was to build a fully electric car much better than the ones that were availible, they also wanted to make it commercially availible. To date Teslas are sold worldwide and help stop the spread of fossil fuels.

GreenPeace

Activist Organization

Greenpeace is a non-governmental environmental organization with offices in over 39 countries and with an international coordinating body in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Greenpeace was founded in 1971 by Irving Stowe and Dorothy Stowe, Canadian and US ex-pat environmental activists.
