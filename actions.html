Which One Are You?

"I'm Batman"-Isaac Hall/2019

How do you feel about climate change? Well then ask yourself how willing are you to help stop it. Use this section to determine what is the best choice for you to help stop the climate change horror. If you use our website you will be able to determine how environmentally friendly you are. By looking in this section you are faced with different guidelines by doing the easy you care for the environment but cant do much to help, if you choose the medium section you are willing to change your lifestyle to help your environment and if you choose the hard you are willing to stop what you want and only care for your environment.
